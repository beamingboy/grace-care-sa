Frequently Asked questions

What is the NDIS?

The National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS) is an Australian government initiative designed to provide support and assistance to individuals with disabilities. It aims to enhance the quality of life for people with disabilities by offering funding for necessary services and supports.

The NDIS takes a person-centered approach, tailoring support plans to meet the unique needs and goals of each participant. It covers a wide range of services, including healthcare, education, employment support, and daily living assistance, to enable individuals with disabilities to participate fully in their communities.
